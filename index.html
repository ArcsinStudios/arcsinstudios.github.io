(2025/07/12) Yet another...?

Us again.
Today's work: Chinese (Simplified) localization.
Check >here< for the results.
Mention: since we use an 8x8 size font, if it's not showing Latin letters, a LARGE amount of text won't be suitable for LONG time reading.
If you're trying to display characters with complicated structure, such as characters in CJK Unified Ideographs:
It is suggested to show a small (or at least, not too large) amount of text or design the text to be short enough to be read in 4-5 seconds.
